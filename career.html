Primary Hiring

Native English Teacher

Are you a native English speaker passionate about education and cultural exchange? Join our vetted network of professional teachers placing in top Indonesian schools across Java and beyond.

Requirements

  • Native English speaker (UK, US, AUS, CAN, NZ, IRE, SA)
  • TEFL, CELTA, or equivalent certification preferred
  • Minimum 1 year classroom or tutoring experience
  • Clean background check
  • Valid passport — visa support provided

What We Offer

  • Competitive monthly salary (IDR-based)
  • Accommodation support & airport pickup
  • Curriculum framework & lesson plans provided
  • Ongoing training, observations & feedback
  • Community of expat teachers across Indonesia
🤝
Primary Hiring

Local Assistant Teacher

Bilingual Indonesian educators who co-facilitate programs alongside native teachers, manage classroom logistics, and provide the cultural bridge that makes learning click for students.

Requirements

  • Fluent English speaker — minimum B2 CEFR level
  • Education, English literature, or teaching background preferred
  • Comfortable working with native English speakers
  • Warm, patient, and classroom-savvy personality

What We Offer

  • Part-time & full-time options available
  • Flexible scheduling around school sessions
  • Professional development & CPD workshops
  • Career growth into senior facilitation roles
  • Exposure to international teaching methods
