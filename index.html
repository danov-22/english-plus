Who We Are

English Plus is a professional English extracurricular agency dedicated to bringing high-quality, native-led English programs into Indonesian schools. We bridge the gap between international-standard English instruction and local school environments.

Our Mission

To equip every student with the confidence and competence to communicate fluently in English — through structured, engaging, and culturally responsive programs.
