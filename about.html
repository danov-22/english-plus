Who We Are

English Plus is a professional English extracurricular agency dedicated to bringing high-quality, native-led English programs into Indonesian schools. We bridge the gap between international-standard English instruction and local school environments.

Our Mission

To equip every student with the confidence and competence to communicate fluently in English — through structured, engaging, and culturally responsive programs.

Our Vision

To become Indonesia's most trusted school English partner, known for measurable student outcomes, seamless school integration, and world-class native teacher placement.

💬

Our Teaching Philosophy

Communicative competence first. We believe language is learned through real use — conversation, storytelling, debate, and play — not rote memorization.

🤝

Why Schools Choose Us

We handle everything — teacher recruitment, vetting, training, scheduling, curriculum design, and progress reporting — so schools can focus on what they do best.

Our Process

From First Call to Thriving Classroom

A seamless, end-to-end partnership process built around your school's needs.

01

Consultation

We meet with your principal or academic coordinator to understand your school's goals, student levels, schedule requirements, and curriculum alignment needs.

02

Program Design

Our curriculum team creates a custom program plan tailored to your school — selecting the right programs, level structures, materials, and learning outcomes.

03

Teacher Placement

We match your school with the most suitable native and local assistant teachers based on program type, student age group, and school culture fit.

04

Weekly Classes Begin

Programs launch on schedule. Our operations team monitors attendance, quality, and teacher performance — keeping everything running smoothly every week.

05

Progress Reporting

Schools receive detailed student progress reports every term, with individual feedback, skill level assessments, and program performance summaries.
