Questions

Everything you need to know about partnering with English Plus. Can't find your answer? Contact us directly.

We work with private schools, international schools, national-plus schools, and Islamic schools across Indonesia. Our programs are adaptable to any school structure and student demographic, from SD through SMA.

Every native teacher undergoes a multi-stage process including application review, demo teaching evaluation, background checks, visa and permit verification, and a comprehensive onboarding training. We also conduct ongoing classroom observations.

Absolutely. During the consultation phase, we work with your team to select and customize the programs that best fit your school's vision, student age groups, and available time slots. There's no one-size-fits-all package.

Reports are delivered each term and include individual skill assessments (speaking, listening, vocabulary, pronunciation), participation scores, teacher notes, and a summary of program-wide learning outcomes. Reports can be shared with parents directly.

We recommend groups of 12–20 students for optimal interaction. Larger groups can be accommodated with additional local assistant teacher support. Class sizes are finalized during the program design phase.

Yes. English Plus provides all curriculum frameworks, session plans, worksheets, and supplementary materials for every program. Schools only need to supply a suitable classroom space and basic AV equipment.

Our standard partnership is one academic semester, which allows students enough time to see meaningful progress. We also offer full-year and multi-year partnership arrangements with enhanced reporting and program development benefits.

English Plus currently operates across Java, with active partnerships in Jakarta, Bogor, Tangerang, Bekasi, Bandung, Surabaya, and Yogyakarta. We are expanding to other major cities — contact us to discuss availability in your area.

Pricing depends on the program type, session frequency, number of students, and whether a local assistant teacher is included. We provide a detailed proposal after the initial consultation. There are no hidden fees — everything is transparent and agreed upfront.

Yes. At the end of each term, schools receive individual student reports that can be distributed to parents. Some partner schools choose to share these directly via their school apps or parent-teacher meetings. We can tailor the report format to suit your parent communication style.
